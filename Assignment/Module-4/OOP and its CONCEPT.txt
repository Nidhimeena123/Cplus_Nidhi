What is OOP?
-OOP stands for Object-Oriented Programming which uses objects, classes, and other related concepts to structure and organize code. 
- Here are some concepts in Object-Oriented Programming:
1. Class
2. Object
3. Encapsulation
4. Inheritance
5. Polymorphism
6. Abstraction 
7. Method Overloading
8. Method Overriding
9. association
10. Composition
